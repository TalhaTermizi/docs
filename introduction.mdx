## What you can do with eInvoices

eInvoices covers every stage of the invoicing lifecycle:

- **Create invoices** — Build polished invoices with your logo, brand colors, custom line items, taxes, and discounts.
- **Send & track** — Email invoices to clients and see exactly when they're opened, paid, or overdue.
- **Collect payments** — Accept credit cards, bank transfers, and more through integrated payment gateways.
- **Automate** — Set up recurring invoices and automatic payment reminders so nothing slips through the cracks.
- **Report** — Generate revenue summaries, tax reports, and outstanding balance overviews at any time.
- **Integrate** — Connect to accounting software like QuickBooks or Xero, or build custom workflows with the REST API and webhooks.
