# Welcome to ezInvoice

ezInvoice is a ZATCA Phase 2 compliant e-invoicing platform for businesses in Saudi Arabia. Our API handles invoice clearance, reporting, and document management so you can integrate e-invoicing directly into your ERP or POS system.

## What you can do

- Clear standard (B2B) invoices in real-time with ZATCA
- Report simplified (B2C) invoices
- Generate and retrieve invoices, credit notes, and debit notes
- Get PDF and XML versions of any document
