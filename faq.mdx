Whether you're just getting started or managing a growing business, you'll find clear answers to the most common eInvoices questions below. If you don't see what you're looking for, reach out to our support team at support@einvoices.com or use the in-app chat.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Is eInvoices free to use?">
    eInvoices offers a free plan that lets you send up to 5 invoices per month with core features included — no credit card required to sign up. When your invoicing needs grow, you can upgrade to a paid plan to unlock unlimited invoices, team access, custom branding, and advanced reporting. Visit the Pricing page on the eInvoices website to compare all available plans side by side.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Go to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan** and select the plan that fits your needs. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades are scheduled for your next renewal date, so you keep your current plan's features until then. You can also cancel or pause your subscription from the same page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I accept payments in multiple currencies?">
    Yes — eInvoices supports multi-currency invoicing on the Pro and Business plans. When you create an invoice, open the **Currency** dropdown and select the currency your client will pay in. Each invoice can carry its own currency, so you can bill different clients in different currencies within the same account. Keep in mind that your payment gateway must also support the currency you choose; check your gateway's documentation to confirm which currencies are enabled.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I add my company logo to invoices?">
    Navigate to **Settings → Branding** and upload your logo under the **Company Logo** section — PNG or SVG files at 500 × 500 px or larger work best for sharp PDF output. Once saved, your logo appears automatically on all new invoices and on the client payment portal. To update the logo on previously sent invoices, re-send them from the invoice detail page after saving your new branding.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods can my clients use?">
    The payment methods available to your clients depend on which payment gateways you've connected in **Settings → Integrations**. With Stripe enabled, clients can pay by credit card, debit card, and — where available — local methods such as SEPA Direct Debit or iDEAL. Connecting PayPal lets clients pay via their PayPal balance or linked bank account. You can enable multiple gateways at once so clients see all their options on the payment page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I void or delete an invoice?">
    Open the invoice, click the **Actions** menu (the three-dot icon in the top-right corner), and choose either **Void** or **Delete**. Voiding marks the invoice as cancelled while preserving the record in your history — this is the recommended approach for audit compliance. Deleting permanently removes the invoice and cannot be undone, so only delete invoices that were created by mistake and never sent to a client.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I customize the invoice PDF layout?">
    Yes — eInvoices includes several built-in PDF templates you can switch between under **Settings → Branding → Invoice Template**. On the Pro plan and above, you can also adjust the column layout, toggle specific fields on or off (such as the tax number or payment terms), and choose a custom accent colour to match your brand. Full custom HTML/CSS templates are available on the Business plan; contact support to have yours reviewed and activated.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does eInvoices file taxes on my behalf?">
    eInvoices calculates and displays tax amounts on your invoices based on the tax rates you configure in **Settings → Taxes**, but it does not file or remit taxes to any tax authority on your behalf. You remain responsible for reporting and paying taxes in your jurisdiction. We recommend consulting a local accountant or tax advisor to make sure your tax rates and reporting obligations are set up correctly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I give team members access to my account?">
    Go to **Settings → Team** and click **Invite Member**. Enter your team member's email address and select the role that matches their responsibilities — **Admin**, **Accountant**, or **Viewer**. They'll receive an invitation email with a link to set up their own password. You can update roles or revoke access at any time from the same Team settings page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?">
    When you cancel, your account moves to a read-only state for 30 days. During that window you can still log in, view all your invoices and client records, and export your data. After 30 days, your data is scheduled for permanent deletion in accordance with our Privacy Policy (available on the eInvoices website). To avoid any data loss, export everything you need before the 30-day window closes — see the next question for instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import existing invoices or clients?">
    Yes — eInvoices accepts CSV imports for both clients and invoice line items. Go to **Clients → Import** or **Invoices → Import**, download the provided CSV template, fill in your data following the column definitions in the template, then upload the completed file. The importer validates your data row by row and flags any errors before committing the import, so you can fix issues without losing your progress. For large migrations, our support team can assist with bulk imports.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I export all my data?">
    Navigate to **Settings → Data & Privacy → Export Account Data** and click **Request Export**. eInvoices will compile a ZIP archive containing all your invoices (as PDFs and a CSV summary), your client list, payment records, and account settings. You'll receive an email with a secure download link within 24 hours — on most accounts it arrives in minutes. The download link remains active for 7 days.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
