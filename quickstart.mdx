eInvoices gets you from sign-up to a sent, professional invoice in just a few steps. Follow this guide to create your account, set up your business profile, add your first client, and deliver your first invoice — all without touching a spreadsheet or a Word template.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have the following ready:

- A free eInvoices account — [sign up at app.einvoices.com/signup](https://app.einvoices.com/signup) if you don't have one yet.
- Access to the email inbox you used to register, so you can complete email verification.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up and Verify Your Email">
    Create your account and confirm your identity so your invoices are sent from a trusted, verified address.

    1. Go to [app.einvoices.com/signup](https://app.einvoices.com/signup) and enter your name, email address, and a strong password.
    2. Click **Create Account**. eInvoices immediately sends a verification email to the address you provided.
    3. Open the email — look for a message from **no-reply@einvoices.com** with the subject line *Verify your eInvoices email address*.
    4. Click the **Verify Email** button inside the message. You are redirected back to the eInvoices dashboard and your account is now active.

    <Note>
      If the verification email doesn't arrive within a couple of minutes, check your spam or junk folder. You can also request a new email from the **Resend Verification** link on the login page.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Business Profile">
    Your business profile powers the "From" section of every invoice you send. Filling it out now means every future invoice looks polished and professional without any extra effort.

    1. In the left sidebar, click **Settings**, then select **Business Profile**.
    2. Enter your **Company Name** exactly as it should appear on invoices and client communications.
    3. Fill in your **Business Address** — include street, city, state/province, postal code, and country.
    4. Upload your **Company Logo** by clicking **Upload Logo** and selecting a PNG or JPG file (max 2 MB). Your logo appears in the top-left corner of every invoice.
    5. Choose your **Default Currency** from the dropdown (for example, USD, EUR, or GBP). You can override this per invoice if you bill international clients.
    6. Click **Save Profile** to apply your changes.

    <Tip>
      Pick a square or landscape logo with a transparent background for the cleanest results on both white and branded invoice templates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Client">
    eInvoices stores your client details so you can address future invoices in seconds. Add a client now and it becomes available to every invoice you create going forward.

    1. In the left sidebar, click **Clients**, then click **+ New Client**.
    2. Enter the client's **Full Name** or **Company Name**.
    3. Add the client's **Email Address** — this is where eInvoices delivers the invoice PDF.
    4. Fill in the client's **Billing Address**, including country. This appears in the "Bill To" section of the invoice.
    5. Optionally, add a **Phone Number** and any **Internal Notes** (these notes are private and never shown to the client).
    6. Click **Save Client**.

    Your new client now appears in the **Clients** list and is immediately available to select when building an invoice.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create and Send Your First Invoice">
    With your profile complete and your first client saved, you're ready to generate and deliver a real invoice.

    1. In the left sidebar, click **Invoices**, then click **+ New Invoice**.
    2. In the **Client** field, start typing your client's name and select them from the dropdown.
    3. Confirm or adjust the **Invoice Date** and **Due Date** (or select a payment term such as Net 30).
    4. Under **Line Items**, click **+ Add Item** and enter a description, quantity, and unit price for each service or product you're billing for.
    5. If applicable, apply a **Tax Rate** using the tax toggle — eInvoices calculates the total automatically.
    6. Add an optional **Note to Client** (for example, payment instructions or a thank-you message) in the notes field at the bottom.
    7. Click **Preview** to see exactly what your client will receive, then click **Send Invoice** to deliver it to your client's inbox instantly.

    <Note>
      eInvoices marks the invoice as **Sent** and starts tracking it. You'll receive a notification when your client opens the email, and another when the invoice is marked paid.
    </Note>

    Congratulations — you've just sent your first professional invoice with eInvoices! 🎉
  </Step>
</Steps>
